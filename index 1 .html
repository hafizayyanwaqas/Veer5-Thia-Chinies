Have Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What type of cuisine does Veera 5 offer?

+

Veera 5 specializes in authentic Thai and Chinese cuisine. Our menu features a wide range of dishes including Thai curries, Pad Thai, Tom Yum soups, Chinese stir-fries, dim sum, noodles, fried rice, and many more traditional Asian dishes prepared by our expert chefs.

Do you offer delivery services?

+

Yes! We offer delivery services across DHA Lahore and surrounding areas. You can place your order via WhatsApp at 0304-4999829 or through popular food delivery apps. We also offer takeaway for customers who prefer to pick up their orders.

What are your operating hours?

+

We are open 7 days a week from 12:00 PM to 12:00 AM (midnight). During Ramadan and special occasions, timings may vary. Please call ahead for the latest hours during holidays.

Can I make a reservation for a private event?

+

Absolutely! We accommodate birthday parties, anniversary celebrations, corporate dinners, and other private events. Please contact us at 0304-4999829 to discuss your event requirements. We offer customized menus and special decorations for your occasions.

Do you have vegetarian options?

+

Yes! We have a dedicated vegetarian section on our menu featuring dishes like Tofu Green Curry, Mapo Tofu, Stir-Fried Mixed Vegetables, Vegetable Spring Rolls, and more. We can also customize many dishes to accommodate vegetarian and vegan preferences.

Is parking available?

+

Yes, convenient parking is available near our restaurant in DHA Phase 5. We're located at Building no 80, Street 6, which has accessible street parking and nearby commercial parking areas.
